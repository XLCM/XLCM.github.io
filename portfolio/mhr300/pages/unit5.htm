This Unit is about Human Resources Management

Many organizations’ biggest investment is in people; human resources make up the majority of all expenditures made by organizations for operations. Yet many companies fail to leverage and use these resources to their fullest potential because of poor management. Those who figure out how to manage this investment have a great opportunity to gain competitive advantage.

At the end of this Unit, you should be able to:

Know:

  • describe how HR supports organizational strategy
  • describe how to manage human capital through HR practices
  • discuss employer and employee legal and moral rights
  • explain how to motivate and engage employees
  • explain the value of diversity in organizations
  • describe common conflict management styles and discuss the appropriateness of each for a given context
  • identify and discuss the implications of a global context for human resource management

Do:

  • select appropriate HR practice options for a given context
  • design a plan to motivate high job performance in a given context
  • select appropriate conflict management styles in a given context
  • apply HR practices to leverage diversity in a given context
  • identify appropriate metrics to measure HR effectiveness for a given context
  • evaluate employee data to guide HR practice decisions in a given context

Be / Inspire / Network:

  • identify your dominant and non-dominant conflict handling style
  • evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of different conflict handling styles
  • develop strategies for managing those with different conflict handling styles than your own
  • examine the possible risks and rewards of using conflict handling styles that are not your preferred style
  • evaluate strategies for managing others with different conflict handling styles

  2. Rewarding A While Hoping for B for the Accounts Receivable job at Atlas
    1. Describe/explain two ways in which the current reward system at Atlas is rewarding behavior “A” while hoping for behavior “B.”

    1. Recommend whether the Atlas compensation system should use individual-based incentives, team-based incentives, or a combination of the two for the Accounts Receivable job.
    2. Briefly explain how/why your recommendation for team versus individual incentives should improve the overall performance of the Accounts Receivable employees at Atlas.
